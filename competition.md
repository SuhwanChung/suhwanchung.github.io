### [Predicting Deforestation Region in Indonesia](https://datameka.com/competition/632c46a4-9c05-4911-a8c2-08226d2fb4e4)

---
> Identify key drivers of deforestation and provide insights that can help shape sustainable policies

Deforestation is a significant environmental problem worldwide, and Indonesia is no exception. The country has one of the highest deforestation rates globally, with over 28 million hectares of forest lost between 2001 and 2021.
In recent years, satellite imagery has been used to monitor and track deforestation, and deep learning techniques have shown promise in accurately identifying and classifying factors of deforestation.<br/> 
__This competition aims to harness the power of deep learning to classify factors of deforestation in Indonesia using satellite imagery.__

### [Detecting Anomalies in Manufacturing Plants](https://datameka.com/competition/3e9f027f-f880-4882-a0bf-f081adcaa421)

---
> Create an anomaly factory sound detection model using machinery sound data

The manufacturing sector has experienced remarkable growth in recent years. 
However, with the increasing number of factories, detecting machine breakdowns and safety risks has become a challenge. 
The annual cost of unplanned downtime in the manufacturing sector is estimated to be a substantial amount. 
To address these challenges, factories are exploring machine learning and other technologies for effective anomaly detection. 
Detecting abnormal events in factory sound is particularly important as it provides valuable insights into machine health and performance. In this challenge, participants will develop machine learning models specifically designed to detect anomalies in factory sound data accurately. 
By achieving accurate anomaly detection, participants can help improve operational safety and efficiency, reducing downtime and increasing productivity.
